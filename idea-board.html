Turn a rough idea into a clear, visual plan.

Describe your idea in a sentence and Hey Toolie maps it out — who it is for, the problem it solves, the risks, ways it could make money, a health score, and what to build first. Then reshape any part of it just by asking. No sign-up needed to try it.

How it works

  1. Describe your idea in one or two sentences.
  2. Toolie maps out target users, risks, money ideas, a health score and a visual map.
