Recipe Extractor

Paste any cooking video — get a clean, editable recipe.

Drop in a cooking-video link or paste a wall of recipe text, and Hey Toolie pulls out a beginner-friendly recipe: a tidy ingredient checklist, clear numbered steps, and a ready-to-go shopping list. Then make it yours — scale the servings, swap ingredients, or go dairy-free — just by asking. No sign-up needed to try it.

How it works

  1. Paste a cooking-video link or recipe text.
  2. Toolie pulls out the ingredients, step-by-step method, and a shopping list.
  3. Tweak it your way — scale it, swap ingredients, or simplify the steps by asking Toolie.
